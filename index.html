The Challenge

Mission shouldn’t be limited by financial uncertainty.

  • Financial reporting that arrives late — or never tells the full story
  • Audit season that brings stress instead of confidence
  • Internal controls that are informal, outdated, or undocumented
  • Boards left guessing because the numbers aren’t clear
  • Cash flow uncertainty that makes planning feel impossible
  • Grant and fund compliance that keeps leadership up at night
  • Executive directors stretched thin, carrying finance alone
Our Role

We bring the steady hand of a senior financial leader to organizations that can’t justify — or afford — a full-time controller. Clean books. Board-grade reporting. Real internal controls. The clarity that lets leaders return their attention to the mission itself.

About the Founder

A trusted steward of mission and money.

For more than 27 years, LaCrecia Williams has carried the financial weight that allows mission-driven organizations to do their work with confidence. Her career spans the most demanding environments in nonprofit and philanthropic finance — including controller-level leadership and senior financial management inside a major community foundation.

She has owned the month-end close, prepared organizations for audit, stood before boards with numbers they could trust, and stewarded restricted funds, grants, and investment reporting with the care those dollars deserve. She built Stewardship Financial Group on a simple conviction: every organization advancing a mission deserves the financial leadership usually reserved for those who can afford a full-time executive.

She is, first, a steward — of mission, of trust, and of the resources placed in good hands. That is the standard she brings to every organization she serves.
